About Summit Detailing

Summit Detailing was started by Freakbob Squarepants! Being a car fanatic, he wanted to bring his love for cars to the Bikini Bottom community. Freakbob has been detailing cars for over 10 years and has a passion for making cars look their best. Summit Detailing is the #1 Auto Detailing service in Bikini Bottom! Freakbob provides mobile detailing, bringing the clean to you! He can't drive though so he drags a wagon of his equipment to you. Once he figures out how to drive, he has a MK4 Supra waiting for him in his garage.
